Applied Metric Geometry and Topology Seminar

Organization

Times: Thursday 4pm to 6pm

Location: MW 154.

Contact: Facundo Mémoli.

Description: A reading group for undergrads and graduate students about Applications of Metric Geometry and Topology to Data Analysis.
